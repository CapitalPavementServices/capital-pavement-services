About Bumper Block & Post Installation

Parking bumper blocks and bollards are essential components of a safe, well-organized parking lot. Bumper blocks protect buildings, storefronts, curbs, landscaping, utilities, and pedestrian areas from vehicle encroachment. Bollards control traffic flow, prevent unauthorized vehicle access, and protect high-value infrastructure. Capital Pavement Services provides professional bumper block and post installation services for commercial parking lots, HOAs, and municipalities throughout Northern Virginia — both new installations and replacements for damaged or missing units.

Proper installation is critical. Bumper blocks that are installed without anchor pins or set in improperly prepared asphalt shift over time, eventually becoming loose and creating serious tripping hazards for pedestrians. Bollards require proper footings or anchoring to perform their protective function. Our team installs concrete parking curbs and bollards to manufacturer specifications, with appropriate anchoring for your pavement type and traffic conditions. We also assess existing bumper block installations to identify units that have shifted and pose hazard risks.

What’s Included

  • Precast concrete bumper block (parking stop) installation
  • Steel and concrete bollard installation
  • Anchor pin installation and anchoring into asphalt
  • Concrete footing installation for bollards where required
  • Removal and disposal of damaged or missing units
  • New parking lot bumper block layout planning
  • Replacement of shifted or loose bumper blocks
  • Painted yellow ends and reflective hardware
  • Post-installation inspection for proper seating

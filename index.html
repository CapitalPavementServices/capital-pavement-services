Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about parking lot striping, sealcoating, ADA compliance, and pavement maintenance in Northern Virginia.

How often should a parking lot be sealcoated?

Most commercial parking lots in Northern Virginia should be sealcoated every 2–3 years. The right schedule depends on traffic volume, sun exposure, and the current condition of your asphalt. Freshly paved surfaces should cure for at least 6–12 months before the first application. Regular sealcoating prevents UV oxidation, water infiltration, and fuel spill damage — extending pavement life significantly and reducing long-term repair costs.

What is the difference between parking lot striping and line striping?

Parking lot striping and line striping refer to the same service — the painted markings applied to a parking lot surface, including space lines, directional arrows, fire lanes, handicap symbols, crosswalks, and stall numbers. "Line striping" is a broader industry term covering any pavement line marking, while "parking lot striping" specifically describes commercial lot markings. At Capital Pavement Services, we use both terms to describe our pavement marking work throughout Northern Virginia.

Do you offer ADA compliance upgrades for parking lots?

Yes. We provide full ADA compliance upgrades for commercial parking lots throughout Northern Virginia. This includes accessible parking stalls, van-accessible spaces, access aisles, ISA (International Symbol of Accessibility) symbols, curb ramp markings, and ADA-compliant handicap parking signs. We help property owners meet current ADA requirements to avoid penalties and ensure access for every visitor.

Do you offer pavement maintenance contracts?

Yes. Our pavement maintenance contracts are designed for commercial property owners, property managers, HOAs, and municipalities who want to protect their pavement investment long-term. A contract typically includes scheduled sealcoating, crack filling, restriping, and annual inspections. Contract clients receive priority scheduling and preferred pricing on all services.

How long does parking lot striping take?

Most standard commercial parking lot striping jobs in Northern Virginia are completed in a few hours to a single day, depending on lot size and layout complexity. Freshly painted lines typically dry within 30–60 minutes under normal weather conditions. We schedule work to minimize disruption to your tenants and customers — early mornings, evenings, and weekends are all available.

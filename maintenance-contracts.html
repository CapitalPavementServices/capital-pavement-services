About Pavement Maintenance Contracts

The most economical way to protect your pavement investment is through a proactive, scheduled maintenance program — not reactive repairs after deterioration has already advanced. Every dollar invested in timely crack filling and sealcoating saves multiple dollars in future repair costs. Capital Pavement Services offers custom pavement maintenance contracts for commercial property owners, property management companies, HOAs, and municipalities throughout Northern Virginia who want to maximize the lifespan of their pavement assets and maintain their properties at a consistently high standard.

Our maintenance contracts are customized to your specific properties, pavement conditions, and budget. A typical maintenance program includes a baseline condition assessment, sealcoating on a 2-to-4-year cycle, annual or bi-annual crack filling, periodic restriping as needed, and regular lot inspections to catch emerging issues early. Contract clients receive priority scheduling — ensuring your work gets done at the optimal time of year — along with preferred pricing, detailed service records for your property files, and a single point of contact for all pavement services.

What’s Included

  • Initial pavement condition assessment for all covered properties
  • Customized multi-year maintenance schedule
  • Sealcoating on recommended 2–4-year cycle
  • Annual or bi-annual crack filling treatments
  • Periodic restriping as markings fade
  • Regular lot inspections with written condition reports
  • Priority scheduling for all services
  • Preferred contract pricing
  • Detailed service records for property management and budgeting
  • Emergency repair response for contract clients

Key Benefits

  • Extends pavement life significantly — often doubling lifespan

About Sealcoating

Sealcoating is the single most cost-effective step you can take to protect your asphalt pavement. Without a quality sealcoat, asphalt is continuously exposed to UV oxidation that causes it to dry out and crack, water infiltration that undermines the base, and fuel and oil spills that dissolve the asphalt binder. Capital Pavement Services provides professional asphalt sealcoating services for commercial parking lots, HOA roadways, and private properties throughout Northern Virginia, using premium coal tar and asphalt emulsion sealers applied with professional equipment.

Our sealcoating process begins with thorough surface preparation — cleaning debris, pressure washing oil spots, applying oil spot primer, and filling existing cracks before the sealer is ever applied. Skipping these prep steps is why sealcoating from inexperienced contractors fails early. We apply sealer in even, consistent coverage using squeegee or spray methods selected based on your pavement’s condition and texture. The result is a uniform, deep black surface that looks like new asphalt and is protected for years to come.

What’s Included

  • Thorough pavement cleaning and debris removal
  • Oil and fuel spot priming
  • Crack filling prior to sealing
  • Professional squeegee or spray sealer application
  • Edge detailing along curbs and borders
  • Two-coat application where warranted
  • Traffic control and curing coordination
  • Reopen-to-traffic scheduling and communication
  • Application to parking lots, driveways, and private roads

Key Benefits

  • Doubles pavement lifespan vs. unsealed asphalt
  • Shields against UV rays, water, and fuel spills
  • Restores like-new deep black appearance
  • Far less expensive than repair or replacement
  • Reduces long-term maintenance and repair costs

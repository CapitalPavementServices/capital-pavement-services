Parking Lot Striping & Line Striping

Precise, long-lasting parking lot striping and paving line striping for commercial and municipal properties throughout Northern Virginia.

About Parking Lot Striping

Fresh, professionally applied parking lot striping does more than improve your property’s appearance — it maximizes available parking space, directs traffic flow, reduces accidents, and keeps your property legally compliant. Capital Pavement Services provides expert parking lot striping and line striping for commercial properties, retail centers, office parks, HOAs, schools, hospitals, and municipalities throughout Northern Virginia. Whether you need a complete new layout or a restripe of existing markings, we deliver crisp, precise results that last.

Our paving line striping services use high-quality traffic paint — both waterborne and solvent-based formulations selected based on your pavement type and traffic conditions — applied with professional striping machines for consistent line width and sharp edges. We also provide layout consulting for properties undergoing construction or renovation, ensuring your parking lot design maximizes space and meets local code requirements before the first line is painted.

What’s Included

  • Parking stall striping (standard and ADA-accessible)
  • Fire lane and curb striping
  • Directional arrows and chevrons
  • Crosswalk and pedestrian pathway markings
  • No-parking zone and loading zone markings
  • Handicap stall and access aisle striping
  • New parking lot layout design and consulting
  • Restriping of faded or worn markings
  • Layout compliance review for local codes

Key Benefits

  • Maximizes available parking space
  • Improves traffic flow and reduces accidents
  • Meets ADA and local code requirements
  • Crisp lines that stay visible through all seasons
  • Minimal downtime — quick dry time returns lot to use

About ADA Compliance Upgrades & Markings

ADA compliance in your parking lot is a federal legal requirement — not optional, and not something to defer. The Americans with Disabilities Act requires a specific number of accessible parking spaces based on your lot’s total capacity, precise dimensional standards for accessible stalls and access aisles, and specific pavement markings including the International Symbol of Accessibility. Non-compliance exposes your property to federal enforcement, private lawsuits, and significant liability. Capital Pavement Services provides comprehensive ADA compliance upgrades and pavement marking services for commercial properties and municipalities throughout Northern Virginia.

Our ADA compliance services begin with an assessment of your current parking lot against ADA Standards for Accessible Design and applicable local requirements. We identify gaps — insufficient accessible stall counts, undersized access aisles, missing or faded markings, improper van-accessible spaces — and recommend the most efficient and compliant solution. We then perform all required striping, stenciling, and signage upgrades to bring your property into full compliance. We stay current on ADA requirements so you don’t have to.

What’s Included

  • ADA compliance assessment of existing lot layout
  • Accessible parking stall striping (standard 8’ wide with 5’ access aisle)
  • Van-accessible parking stall striping (8’ wide with 8’ access aisle)
  • International Symbol of Accessibility (ISA) stenciling
  • “Van Accessible” and “Reserved” pavement text markings
  • Curb ramp identification and approach markings
  • Accessible route pavement markings
  • Signage recommendations and installation
  • ADA stall count verification per lot capacity
  • Documentation of completed compliance work

Key Benefits

  • Protects against costly DOJ enforcement and private suits
  • Welcoming and accessible to all customers and visitors
  • Complete service from assessment through installation
  • Stay current with ADA Standards for Accessible Design

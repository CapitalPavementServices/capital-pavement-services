About Asphalt Paving & Repair

Whether you need a single pothole repaired, a section of failing pavement rebuilt, or a brand-new parking lot constructed, Capital Pavement Services delivers professional asphalt paving and repair results throughout Northern Virginia. We handle projects of all sizes, from small commercial property repairs to large-scale new construction for developers and municipalities. Our team has the equipment, materials knowledge, and field experience to assess each situation correctly and deliver paving and repair work that holds up for years.

Our asphalt repair process starts with a thorough assessment to identify root causes — not just surface symptoms. Potholes that are simply patched without addressing underlying base failure or drainage issues will fail again quickly. We identify subgrade problems, drainage deficiencies, and structural issues, then develop the right repair approach. We use commercial-grade hot-mix asphalt from reputable suppliers and proper compaction techniques to deliver smooth, durable finished surfaces that integrate cleanly with existing pavement.

What’s Included

  • Pothole patching (temporary and permanent infrared or cold-mix)
  • Full-depth reclamation (FDR) for failing sections
  • Base and subgrade repair and stabilization
  • Utility cut patching
  • Edge repair and edge milling
  • Saw-cutting for clean, defined repair perimeters
  • New parking lot construction and layout
  • Private road and access drive paving
  • Pavement transitions and tie-ins

Key Benefits

  • Root-cause assessment prevents repeat failures
  • Commercial-grade hot-mix asphalt from reputable suppliers
  • Proper compaction for a smooth, durable surface
  • Eliminates liability from potholes and deteriorated pavement
  • Improves property value and curb appeal

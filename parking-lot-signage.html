About Parking Lot Signage

Effective parking lot management requires more than well-maintained pavement and fresh striping — it requires clear, properly installed, code-compliant signage. Parking lot signs direct traffic flow, communicate rules and restrictions, define reserved spaces, protect fire lanes, and create the organized, professional appearance that commercial properties require. Capital Pavement Services supplies and installs a complete range of parking lot signage for commercial properties, HOAs, retail centers, and municipalities throughout Northern Virginia.

We handle everything from single sign replacements to complete signage packages for new parking lot construction and renovation projects. All signs we install meet MUTCD (Manual on Uniform Traffic Control Devices) standards and applicable local requirements. Hardware is commercial grade — properly sized posts, correct mounting heights, and secure anchoring that holds through wind and weather. We frequently coordinate parking lot signage installation alongside our striping and pavement marking services for a complete, finished lot.

What’s Included

  • Stop and yield signs
  • Speed limit signs
  • One-way and directional traffic signs
  • Reserved parking signs
  • No-parking, no-standing, and tow-away zone signs
  • ADA-compliant handicap parking signs (R7-8)
  • Fire lane signs and fire hydrant zone signs
  • Private property and entrance/exit signs
  • Sign post supply and installation
  • Replacement of damaged, faded, or missing signs

Key Benefits

  • Improves traffic flow and reduces on-site accidents
  • Communicates rules clearly to reduce disputes
  • ADA-compliant handicap signs meet federal requirements
  • Commercial-grade hardware built to last outdoors
  • Coordinated with line striping for a complete lot finish

Who We Are

About Capital Pavement Services

We are a full-service pavement marking and maintenance company dedicated to delivering professional results to commercial and municipal clients throughout Northern Virginia.

Our Mission

Built on Quality, Reliability & Professionalism

Capital Pavement Services was founded on a straightforward principle: deliver the quality of work that commercial property owners in Northern Virginia deserve. Pavement is one of the most visible — and most used — features of any commercial property. A well-maintained parking lot communicates professionalism and care. A neglected one communicates the opposite.

We bring that same level of care to every service we provide — from a simple parking lot restriping to a complete pavement overhaul including sealcoating, crack filling and sealing, asphalt paving and repair, bumper block installation, ADA compliance upgrades, and parking lot signage.

Our mission is to be the most dependable pavement services partner in Northern Virginia. That means showing up when we say we will, communicating clearly, using quality materials, and standing behind our work.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every project we take on reflects these values — from the initial estimate through project completion and follow-up.

Quality Workmanship

We use commercial-grade materials and proven techniques on every job. Parking lot striping, sealcoating, asphalt paving, crack filling — every service is performed to the highest standard.

Reliability & Punctuality

We show up when we say we will, finish on schedule, and communicate proactively. Your time and your property operations matter — we respect both.

Customer-First Service

Every client — from a single property owner to a large property management company — receives attentive, responsive service and a clear explanation of work performed.

Integrity & Transparency

Our estimates are honest and detailed. We won’t recommend services you don’t need, and we won’t cut corners on the work you hire us for.

Northern Virginia Roots

Fair & Competitive Pricing
